# KPI Library by Industry (Small to Enterprise)

Use this as a **master KPI catalog**.  
For each business, select:
- **3 North Star KPIs** (outcomes)
- **5 Driver KPIs** (operational levers)
- **2 Guardrail KPIs** (risk/profit protection)

> Tip: Do **not** track everything. Start with 10–15 KPIs, then expand.

---

## Universal KPI Stack (works in any industry)

### Growth
- Revenue Growth % = `(Current Period Revenue - Prior Period Revenue) / Prior Period Revenue`
- New Customer Growth %
- Market Share % (if data available)

### Acquisition
- Leads / Inquiries
- Lead-to-Customer Conversion %
- Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) = `Sales+Marketing Spend / New Customers`

### Monetization
- Average Order Value (AOV) or Average Contract Value (ACV)
- Gross Margin %
- Contribution Margin %

### Retention
- Repeat Purchase Rate %
- Customer Retention Rate %
- Churn Rate %

### Cash & Efficiency
- Cash Conversion Cycle (CCC)
- Days Sales Outstanding (DSO)
- Operating Expense Ratio

### Experience
- NPS
- CSAT
- Complaint Rate / Return Rate

# Company Size Adaptation (Small to Big)

## Small Business (1–50 employees)
- Track **10 KPIs max**
- Weekly cadence
- Prioritize: cash flow, conversion, repeat business, margin
- Keep formulas simple, spreadsheet-friendly

## Mid-Market (51–500)
- Track **15–25 KPIs**
- Weekly + monthly business reviews
- Add: team productivity, channel performance, cohort analysis

## Enterprise (500+)
- Track **25–60 KPIs** across corporate + business units
- Monthly/quarterly governance
- Add: segment profitability, risk/compliance, forecast accuracy, regional scorecards

# KPI Governance Template (copy/paste)

## KPI Dictionary Fields
- KPI Name
- Business Objective
- Formula
- Data Source
- Owner
- Reporting Frequency
- Target
- Green / Yellow / Red Thresholds
- Corrective Action Playbook

## Status Logic
- Green: meets/exceeds target
- Yellow: within 90–99% of target
- Red: below 90% of target (or above threshold for “lower is better” KPIs)

## Review Cadence
- Weekly: Driver KPIs
- Monthly: North Star + Guardrails
- Quarterly: Target reset + KPI pruning/additions
